About DEXPERTS

We're passionate about revolutionizing survey research through innovative automation tools and expert solutions.

Our Story

DEXPERTS was born from a simple observation: survey researchers and programmers were spending countless hours on repetitive, manual tasks that could be automated.

Our flagship XML generation tool was developed to solve a real problem we experienced firsthand. What used to take hours of manual coding now takes seconds, allowing research professionals to focus on what matters most - insights and analysis.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our suite of tools, always with one goal in mind: making survey research more efficient, accurate, and enjoyable.
